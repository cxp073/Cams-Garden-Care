About

A small, considered weekend round.

I'm Cam. I run a solo lawn-care round across Great Preston, Garforth, Kippax and Allerton Bywater — and now a few lawns in Woodlesford. It's a weekend round — and kept that way on purpose.

Working weekends only means I'm selective about the lawns I take on, and that's deliberate. I'd rather do the whole work on a small number of lawns — the cut, the edges, the seasonal renovation — than rush a mow round as many as I can fit in a day.

Same price every time. Same person turning up.

The Work

Cultural practices, done properly.

Natural methods, no harsh chemicals. Two flows of work, side by side: the regular round that keeps a lawn tidy through the season, and the seasonal renovation that keeps it genuinely healthy.

Regular care

  • Mowing with a sharp blade
  • Strimming and edging every visit
  • Manual weeding as needed
  • Feeding when the lawn calls for it

Seasonal work

  • Scarifying to lift moss and thatch
  • Aerating to ease compaction
  • Overseeding to thicken thin patches
  • Topdressing to even out levels
  • Nematodes for chafer and leatherjacket
  • Lime where the soil's acidic

For lawns that want looking after through the whole year, I offer seasonal lawn care plans — weekly visits through the growing season, with cutting, edging, feeding and seasonal work handled as one programme.

And if it's just the feeding you're after, there's lawn feeding and a monthly seaweed feed — three well-timed feeds across the year, natural methods, no harsh chemicals.

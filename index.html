About

A small, considered round.

I'm Cam, and I run a solo garden-care round across Great Preston, Garforth, Kippax and Allerton Bywater, with a few gardens in Woodlesford now too. It's a weekend round, and kept that way on purpose.

Lawns are the heart of what I do — the cut, the edges, the feeding and the seasonal work that keeps the grass healthy underneath. But a garden is more than its lawn, so I also take on hedges, borders, and the general garden work that keeps a place looking cared for right through the year. If you'd like more looking after than the regular round, there are seasonal plans and lawn feeding too.

It's just me, every visit — the same person getting to know your garden, season to season. You'll always know who's turning up, and I'll always know your garden. That's the whole idea, and it's the thing I want to hold onto as I take on more.

Same price every time. Same person turning up.

The Work

Cultural practices, done right.

Natural methods, no harsh chemicals. Three strands of work, side by side: the regular round that keeps a lawn tidy through the season, the seasonal renovation that keeps it genuinely healthy, and the hedges, borders and garden work that look after everything else.

Regular care

  • Mowing with a sharp blade
  • Strimming and edging every visit
  • Manual weeding as needed
  • Feeding when the lawn calls for it

Seasonal work

  • Scarifying to lift moss and thatch
  • Aerating to ease compaction
  • Overseeding to thicken thin patches
  • Topdressing to even out levels
  • Nematodes for chafer and leatherjacket
  • Lime where the soil's acidic

Hedges & garden work

More than the lawn

A garden isn't just its lawn, and I'm set up for the rest of it. Hedges cut and shaped, shrubs pruned at the right time of year for each plant, borders weeded and kept on top of, and the general jobs that pile up over a season — the kind of work that's easy to put off, and makes a real difference once it's done.

  • Hedge cutting and shaping
  • Shrub and border pruning, at the right time for each plant
  • Borders weeded and kept tidy
  • General garden work and one-off jobs

Hedges and garden work are quoted on the day, once I've had a look — every garden's different, and that's the fairest way to price it.

For lawns that want looking after through the whole year, I offer seasonal lawn care plans — weekly visits through the growing season, with cutting, edging, feeding and seasonal work handled as one programme.

And if it's just the feeding you're after, there's lawn feeding and a monthly seaweed feed — three well-timed feeds across the year, natural methods, no harsh chemicals.

Come autumn there's the bigger one-off job too — a full autumn lawn renovation: scarifying, aerating, top dressing and overseeding in one September go, with only a limited number taken on each year.
